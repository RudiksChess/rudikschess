## 🚀 What I'm building

### [Cinco Innovative Solutions](https://cincois.com) &nbsp;·&nbsp; `cincois.com`
> Streamlining operations with AI-powered automation so teams are free to innovate and scale.

- 🤖 **AI voice agents** &mdash; 24/7 client care & lead generation that books appointments and answers complex questions, no human in the loop
- 📊 **Data management & analytics** &mdash; centralize messy data into clear, real-time dashboards that drive decisions
- 🔌 **AI integration** &mdash; wire LLMs into the tools you already use (calendars, databases, CRMs) for instant, automated answers
- 📈 **Business optimization** &mdash; end-to-end AI implementation and marketing optimization

### [CINCO &mdash; Centro de Investigación](https://cinco.org.gt) &nbsp;·&nbsp; `cinco.org.gt`
> *"Pensamiento de Vanguardia para el Futuro de Guatemala"* &mdash; cutting-edge thinking for Guatemala's future.

Independent analysis and strategic ideas to empower the next generation of decision-makers &mdash; with deep, data-driven research on **remittances**, exchange-rate margins, transfer costs, and the forces shaping Guatemala's economy. Findings ship as research articles, live dashboards, and rapid-analysis pieces (*El Pulso*).
